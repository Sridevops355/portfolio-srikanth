My Story

From QA to Cloud.

I'm a Freelance QA & Automation Engineer based in Sydney, with 3+ years of experience including my role as Test Consultant at Testpoint™ (ended Dec 2025). I've built test automation frameworks, run API testing pipelines, and led QA processes across multiple client projects.

Since January 2026, I've been working independently on freelance QA and automation projects — building real-world DevOps and AWS skills alongside client delivery.

Most DevOps engineers learn quality engineering after the fact. I'm coming from the other direction — and that gives me an unfair advantage. I understand failure modes, edge cases, and production risk at a level that pure infrastructure engineers often don't.

My background as an Electronics Engineer (BTech, JNTU) plus an MBA in Entrepreneurship (UBSS) means I connect technical decisions to business outcomes — something I've done at every level of my career.

I'm actively transitioning into AWS Cloud & DevOps Engineering — while staying open to senior QA and automation opportunities along the way.
